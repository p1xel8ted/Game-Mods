[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Automatically switches to the correct tool based on what you're aiming at (cursor/right stick)[/size]
[*][size=3]Works with both mouse and controller[/size]
[*][size=3]Legacy proximity-based mode available as an alternative[/size]
[*][size=3]Selects the highest-tier tool available on your action bar[/size]
[*][size=3]Watering cans with water are prioritised; empty cans are sent to water sources for refilling[/size]
[*][size=3]Each tool type can be individually enabled or disabled[/size]
[*][size=3]Option to disable tool switching on farm tiles[/size]
[*][size=3]Tool switching is automatically disabled during combat[/size]
[*][size=3]Enemy detection with configurable range[/size]
[*][size=3]Adjustable switch cooldown to prevent rapid flickering[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[size=3]All settings can be adjusted in-game by pressing F1 to open the configuration menu. This requires ConfigurationManager, which is included in the [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/30]Sun Haven BepInEx Pack[/url].[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[size=3]Install via Vortex (recommended), or manually place the mod DLL into your BepInEx/plugins folder.[/size]
