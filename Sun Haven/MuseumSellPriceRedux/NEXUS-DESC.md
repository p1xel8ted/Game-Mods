[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[size=3]Updated from the original [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/142]Museum Only Items Sell Price[/url] mod by [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/users/52674526]@Mimikitten[/url].[/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Makes museum-only items worth selling instead of being nearly worthless[/size]
[*][size=3]Adjustable price multiplier via the configuration menu[/size]
[*][size=3]Different item categories sell for gold, orbs, or tickets as appropriate[/size]
[*][size=3]Changes apply instantly — no restart needed, just hit "Apply Changes"[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[size=3]All settings can be adjusted in-game by pressing F1 to open the configuration menu. This requires ConfigurationManager, which is included in the [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/30]Sun Haven BepInEx Pack[/url].[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[size=3]Install via Vortex (recommended), or manually place the mod DLL into your BepInEx/plugins folder.[/size]
