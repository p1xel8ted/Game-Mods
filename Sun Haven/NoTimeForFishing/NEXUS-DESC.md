[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Skip the fishing minigame entirely[/size]
[*][size=3]Automatic reeling with or without the reel animation[/size]
[*][size=3]Instant fish attraction to the bobber[/size]
[*][size=3]Increased bobber attraction radius[/size]
[*][size=3]Faster fish swim speed towards the bobber[/size]
[*][size=3]Adjustable fish spawn rate and population limit[/size]
[*][size=3]Disable the "You caught a fish!" popup[/size]
[*][size=3]Increase odds of catching rarer fish[/size]
[*][size=3]Increase odds of catching museum items while fishing[/size]
[*][size=3]Customise the Bubble Net spell's fish capacity[/size]
[*][size=3]Adjustable rod casting speed and throw distance[/size]
[*][size=3]Adjustable minigame speed and win area size[/size]
[*][size=3]Recommended Settings button to apply suggested defaults[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[size=3]All settings can be adjusted in-game by pressing F1 to open the configuration menu. This requires ConfigurationManager, which is included in the [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/30]Sun Haven BepInEx Pack[/url].[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[size=3]Install via Vortex (recommended), or manually place the mod DLL into your BepInEx/plugins folder.[/size]
