# Changelog

## 0.1.2

- Improved fish attraction speed reliability
- Fixed bobber attraction radius not applying correctly on first cast
- Disabling the fish catch popup now works in all languages, not just English

## 0.1.1

- Minor update for 2.5.1b (no functional changes).

## 0.1.0

- Minor update for 2.0.2c (no functional changes).

## 0.0.9

- KeepAlive is now a hard dependency, meaning the mod will not load without it. The BepInEx log window will notify you accordingly. Do not raise a bug report.

## 0.0.8

- Updated for 1.4+

## 0.0.7

- Added option to modify odds of getting rarer fish. Note, it increases the odds of getting a higher chance to obtain rarer fish.
- Added option to modify odds of getting museum items from fishing. There are only 11 items to obtain from this category. When you load a save game, the ones you have caught/not caught are logged.
- Fixed not getting museum items from fishing when using no nibbles option (the name of this option has changed, check your settings).
