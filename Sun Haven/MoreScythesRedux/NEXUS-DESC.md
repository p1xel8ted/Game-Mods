[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[size=3]Updated from the original [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/188]More Scythes[/url] mod by [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/users/101098328]@Brqdford[/url].[/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Adds four new scythes: Adamant, Mithril, Sunite, and Glorite[/size]
[*][size=3]Adamant, Mithril, and Sunite scythes are crafted at the Anvil[/size]
[*][size=3]Glorite scythe is crafted at the Monster Anvil[/size]
[*][size=3]Each scythe has increasing damage and attack speed[/size]
[*][size=3]Requires the appropriate skill level to use[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[size=3]All settings can be adjusted in-game by pressing F1 to open the configuration menu. This requires ConfigurationManager, which is included in the [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/30]Sun Haven BepInEx Pack[/url].[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[size=3]Install via Vortex (recommended), or manually place the mod DLL into your BepInEx/plugins folder.[/size]
