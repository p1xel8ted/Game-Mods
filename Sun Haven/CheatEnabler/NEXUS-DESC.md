[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Enables the built-in developer console commands without restrictions[/size]
[*][size=3]DLC items cannot be spawned or added — this is intentional[/size]
[*][size=3]Adds helpful commands like /finditemid, /printallnpcs, /printallscenes, and more[/size]
[*][size=3]Type /man followed by a command name to view its manual[/size]
[*][size=3]Command descriptions are available in multiple languages[/size]
[*][size=3]Use the chat window and type / to see all available commands[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[size=3]All settings can be adjusted in-game by pressing F1 to open the configuration menu. This requires ConfigurationManager, which is included in the [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/30]Sun Haven BepInEx Pack[/url].[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[size=3]Install via Vortex (recommended), or manually place the mod DLL into your BepInEx/plugins folder.[/size]
