[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Prevents mods from being unloaded when returning to the main menu[/size]
[*][size=3]Without this mod, you would need to restart the game every time you quit to menu[/size]
[*][size=3]Required by most other Sun Haven mods[/size]
[*][size=3]Included in the Sun Haven BepInEx Pack — no need to install separately if using the pack[/size]
[*][size=3]Modders: Use KeepAlive.Plugin.AddGameObjectToNoKillList() to protect custom GameObjects[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[size=3]Install via Vortex (recommended), or manually place the mod DLL into your BepInEx/plugins folder.[/size]
