[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Adds 6 extra jewelry slots: 2 rings, 2 keepsakes, and 2 amulets[/size]
[*][size=3]Extra jewelry provides stat bonuses just like regular slots[/size]
[*][size=3]Toggle the jewelry pouch panel with the arrow button in your inventory[/size]
[*][size=3]Hidden jewelry still provides its stat bonuses[/size]
[*][size=3]Items in extra slots are saved and persist across sessions[/size]
[*][size=3]Stats from extra jewelry apply immediately when the game loads[/size]
[*][size=3]Smart equipping — checks for available slots before swapping[/size]
[*][size=3]Remembers whether the panel was shown or hidden between sessions[/size]
[*][size=3]Option to make extra slots storage-only (no stat bonuses)[/size]
[*][size=3]Controller navigation support[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[size=3]All settings can be adjusted in-game by pressing F1 to open the configuration menu. This requires ConfigurationManager, which is included in the [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/30]Sun Haven BepInEx Pack[/url].[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[size=3]Install via Vortex (recommended), or manually place the mod DLL into your BepInEx/plugins folder.[/size]
