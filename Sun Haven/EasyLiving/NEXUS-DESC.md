[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Toggle social media buttons, DLC ads, patch notes, and developer logo on the main menu[/size]
[*][size=3]Owned DLC is greyed out in the DLC carousel[/size]
[*][size=3]Toggle the DLC shop button on the main menu[/size]
[*][size=3]Quick Save feature (saves progress, not position) with a configurable keybind[/size]
[*][size=3]Adds a Quit to Desktop button on the in-game settings panel[/size]
[*][size=3]Adjustable quest tracker height[/size]
[*][size=3]Skip the "missing items" dialogue when interacting with museum displays[/size]
[*][size=3]Auto-load your most recent save on startup (hold Left Shift during loading to skip)[/size]
[*][size=3]Adjustable player move speed multiplier (applied before buffs)[/size]
[*][size=3]"Has Materials" selected by default when opening a crafting table[/size]
[*][size=3]Continue button on the main menu to quickly load your last played save[/size]
[*][size=3]Optional health and mana regeneration over time[/size]
[*][size=3]Mute game audio when the window is not focused[/size]
[*][size=3]Player starts away from the mailbox (adjustable)[/size]
[*][size=3]Force resolution update on scene changes (for display issues)[/size]
[*][size=3]Toggle emotes and fast dialogue options[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[size=3]All settings can be adjusted in-game by pressing F1 to open the configuration menu. This requires ConfigurationManager, which is included in the [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/sunhaven/mods/30]Sun Haven BepInEx Pack[/url].[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[size=3]Install via Vortex (recommended), or manually place the mod DLL into your BepInEx/plugins folder.[/size]
