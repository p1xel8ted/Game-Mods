[size=4][b]What[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][size=3]Adds your main display resolution as a resolution option in the menu.[/size]
[*][size=3]Allows zooming in and out using key binds (defaults are + and -) or an interface provided via the F1 key.[/size]
[*][size=3]Control the main HUD scale and positioning on the X &amp; Y axis.[/size]
[*][size=3]Games implementation of VSYNC limits FPS to 60, mod corrects this to your monitors refresh rate.[/size]
[*][size=3]Option to increase physics rate from 60 fps to monitors refresh rate. May or may not notice any difference.[/size]
[*][size=3]Wind effect is scaled correctly. Fog is a WIP as its quite complex (it's not just an overlay).[/size]
[*][size=3]Option to disable Fog in the config (press F1).[/size]
[*][size=3]Main menu artwork scaled to fit.[/size]
[/list]
