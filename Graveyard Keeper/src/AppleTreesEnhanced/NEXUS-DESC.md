[size=4][b]What[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Makes garden apple trees and berry bushes drop their harvest for looting. You can optionally enable world berry bushes; just keep in mind if you don't loot them, eventually, performance will suffer due to all the objects as they seem to stay forever. This can be resolved using Where's Ma Storage.[/size]
[*][size=3]"Realistic" harvest mode. - Randomizes when a harvest drops. It won't drop the instant it's ready now. It's anywhere between 2 and 15 seconds. You can harvest manually if you wish. Harvesting manually nets you the full harvest. If you don't harvest manually, the amount dropped will be between 2 and the maximum for that bush/tree. (3 for bushes, 10 for trees).[/size]
[*][size=3]This also applies to bee hives. A maximum of 1 honey/bee/wax will be dropped in realistic-harvest mode. Full loot if you manually harvest.[/size]
[*][size=3]Bee Keeper will now buy any excess.[/size]
