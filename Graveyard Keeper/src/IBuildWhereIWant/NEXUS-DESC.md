[size=4][b]Notes around crafting[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]If you're not using a multi-inventory/shared inventory mod, the storage for a craft needs to be in the same zone as the crafting station for that station to see the items.[/size]
[*][size=3]If you use a multi-inventory/shared inventory mod, ensure the zone you're building in is included.[/size]
[/list]
[size=4][b]What[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]It enables building pretty much anywhere, not just the zones with a build area.[/size]
[*][size=3]Press Q or LB on a controller to bring up the Craft Anywhere table. It loads every buildable with a remove craft associated with it (otherwise, you wouldn't be able to remove it if you so decided), provided you have unlocked it that playthrough.[/size]
[*][size=3]The keybinds above can be modified in the config.[/size]
[*][size=3]There is a minor delay when opening. It can't be avoided due to the number of items.[/size]
[*][size=3]Some crafts are disabled, i.e. anything refugee-related, as it's just wasted (they can't use it).[/size]
[*][size=3]The yellow construction tape build-grid for zones with it can be turned off in the config.[/size]
[*][size=3]Object collision is still enabled, so you can't build a furnace on your front door. This can be disabled at your own risk![/size]
[/list]
