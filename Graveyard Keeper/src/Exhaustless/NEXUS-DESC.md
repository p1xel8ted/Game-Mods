[list]
[*][size=3]Added Unlimited energy/health due to popular request.[/size]
[*][size=3]Halves the rate at which energy is spent. The game will only show the blue energy spent text once it hits one total energy spent, so you should see this less often.[/size]
[*][size=3]It halves the cost of anything that uses Gratitude[/size]
[*][size=3]*Think* I increased the amount of energy spent before the Tired debuff appeared. It was 300; it's now 1200. Report back otherwise.[/size]
[*][size=3]Compatible with FasterCraft, faster crafts without faster energy loss![/size]
[*][size=3]Sanity spending is now halved.[/size]
[*][size=3]Sleep and meditation time is increased without messing with time scales. (This means the outside world continues as you spend less time sleeping without the mod.[/size]
[*][size=3]Yawns when tired *lol*[/size]
[*][size=3]Automatically wake up at the end of a meditation.[/size]
[*][size=3]Automatically equip another tool if the one you're using breaks. Have to have spares in your inventory.[/size]
[*][size=3]Any newly equipped tools have their durability decrease speed halved. You will need to un-equip and re-equip after reloads.[/size]
[*][size=3]All can be toggled via the config.[/size]
