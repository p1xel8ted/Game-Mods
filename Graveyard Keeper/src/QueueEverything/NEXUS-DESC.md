[size=4][b]What[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Allows almost anything to be queued to craft multiples at once. Some things break when made into a multicraft, so if it won't let you craft, there is a file in the mod directory that records everything you interact with. Send that to me and what you think caused the issue.[/size]
[*][size=3]It can automatically select the maximum amount to craft.[/size]
[*][size=3]Crafts that use multi-quality ingredients will always have the highest quality you have available selected first (doesn't work with Dinner atm)[/size]
[*][size=3]When you change the quantity, the time to cook/craft, the fuel requirement, and the energy requirements all update on-screen according to how many you're crafting instead of the static amount.[/size]
[*][size=3]If you're using FasterCraft and/or Exhaust-less, those values above will be reflected based on your configuration.[/size]
[*][size=3]If a craft is roughly 7 minutes or more, he'll comment about coming back. This can be disabled.[/size]
[*][size=3]If you play with a controller (I do), the big "Craft Now" button will be auto-focused/selected on expanding a multi-craft.[/size]
[*][size=3]"MakeEverythingAuto" option. Note this isn't easy mode. You get approx. half the amount of experience and crafting time is based on energy requirements with a multiplier. It will be slower than using it manually.[/size]
[*][size=3]Most of these things can be toggled in the config.[/size]
[/list]
