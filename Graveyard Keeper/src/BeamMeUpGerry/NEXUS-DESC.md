[size=4][b]What[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Press Z or RB on a controller to bring up the teleport menu (provided you have a teleport stone). [/size]
[*][size=3]The key binds above can be modified. If the mod can't parse the value you entered, it will revert to the defaults above.[/size]
[*][size=3]The cooldown is permanently removed.[/size]
[*][size=3]The custom zones will only appear if you have been to the zone in your current save at least once and talked to their respective NPC for some of them (Mill &amp; Farmer).[/size]
[*][size=3]Gerry will collect a fee of roughly 1% of your current money, up to a maximum of 5%.[/size]
[*][size=3]Gerry and/or his fee can be disabled if you dislike immersion.[/size]
[*][size=3]Option to increase the rollout speed of the menu.[/size]
[/list]
