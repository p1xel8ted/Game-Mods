[size=4][b]Quick Menus[/b][/size]

[size=3]Hotkey-based quick access to four game menus from anywhere - no need to be at the base.[/size]

[size=4][b]Menus[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3][b]F1[/b] - Follower Forms - Browse your follower skin collection[/size]
[*][size=3][b]F2[/b] - Structures - View available structures (browse-only)[/size]
[*][size=3][b]F3[/b] - Clothing - Browse and assign clothing to followers[/size]
[*][size=3][b]F4[/b] - Fleeces - View, equip, and customize fleeces[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3][b]Toggle hotkeys[/b] - Press to open, press again to close[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Quick switch[/b] - Press a different hotkey while a menu is open to switch directly[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Works anywhere[/b] - Open menus from the base, dungeons, or anywhere else[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Configurable[/b] - Rebind all hotkeys and enable/disable each menu individually[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Localized[/b] - Configuration UI and menu titles translated in all 15 supported languages[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cultofthelamb/mods/31]Cult of the Lamb BepInEx 5 Pack[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/p1xel8ted/BepInEx_Configuration_Manager/]BepInEx Configuration Manager[/url] (for in-game configuration)[/size]
[*][size=3]Place the plugin DLL into your "...\Cult of the Lamb\BepInEx\plugins" folder.[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[size=3]All menus are enabled by default. Edit the config file in BepInEx/config/ or use Configuration Manager (F1) to configure in-game.[/size]

[size=3][b]Hotkeys[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][size=3]Follower Forms - Default: F1[/size]
[*][size=3]Build Menu - Default: F2[/size]
[*][size=3]Tailor / Clothing - Default: F3[/size]
[*][size=3]Player Upgrades / Fleeces - Default: F4[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Notes[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]The Structures menu is browse-only - you cannot place buildings from it[/size]
[*][size=3]Fleece customization (left/right to change variants) works while the menu is paused[/size]
[*][size=3]The Clothing menu works even without a Tailor building[/size]
[/list]
