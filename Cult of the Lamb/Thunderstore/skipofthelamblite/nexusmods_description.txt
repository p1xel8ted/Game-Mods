[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[size=3]A lightweight mod that skips developer/publisher splash screens on game startup. No configuration required - just install and play.[/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Automatically skips developer/publisher logo videos when launching the game[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cultofthelamb/mods/31]Cult of the Lamb BepInEx 5 Pack[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Place the plugin DLL into your "...\Cult of the Lamb\BepInEx\plugins" folder.[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3][b]Skip of the Lamb[/b] - Incompatible. Only one variant can be loaded at a time.[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Cult of QoL[/b] - Fully compatible.[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Looking for More?[/b][/size]

[size=3]If you want additional skip features (crown video, Woolhaven cutscenes, boss intros), check out the full [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cultofthelamb/mods/55]Skip of the Lamb[/url] mod instead.[/size]
