[size=4][b]Mass of the Lamb[/b][/size]

[size=3]Standalone mass action mod extracted from Cult of QoL.[/size]

[size=4][b]What It Includes[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Mass follower commands (Bless, Inspire, Bribe, Extort, Intimidate, Romance, Bully, Reassure, Reeducate, Pet, Level Up, Sin Extract)[/size]
[*][size=3]Mass animal actions (Pet, Clean, Feed, Milk, Shear)[/size]
[*][size=3]Mass farm actions (Water, Fertilize, Plant Seeds)[/size]
[*][size=3]Mass fill actions for structures and stations[/size]
[*][size=3]Mass collect actions for beds, shrines, outhouses, compost, totems, poop, and vomit[/size]
[*][size=3]Collect all god tears at once[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Cost System[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Per-action or per-object cost mode[/size]
[*][size=3]Configurable gold and time costs[/size]
[*][size=3]Configurable faith reduction for mass Bless/Inspire[/size]
[*][size=3]Optional cost preview in interaction labels and follower wheel[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cultofthelamb/mods/31]Cult of the Lamb BepInEx 5 Pack[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/p1xel8ted/BepInEx_Configuration_Manager/]BepInEx Configuration Manager[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Place the plugin DLL into your "...\Cult of the Lamb\BepInEx\plugins" folder.[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Notes[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]If both Cult of QoL and Mass of the Lamb are installed, behavior matches the old pre-split setup.[/size]
[*][size=3]If only Cult of QoL is installed, mass actions are no longer included there.[/size]
[/list]
