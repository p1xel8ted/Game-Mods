# Mass of the Lamb

Mass action features extracted from Cult of QoL into a standalone mod.

## Features

### Mass Follower Commands
- Mass Bribe, Bless, Extort, Intimidate, Inspire, Romance
- Mass Bully, Reassure, Reeducate
- Mass Pet Follower (with optional "all followers" mode)
- Mass Level Up (optional instant soul collection)
- Mass Sin Extract
- Configurable notification threshold for summary notifications

### Mass Animal Actions
- Mass Pet Animals
- Mass Clean, Feed, Milk, Shear
- Fill trough to capacity and mass fill troughs

### Mass Farm Actions
- Mass Water, Fertilize, Plant Seeds
- Fill-to-capacity and mass fill for carpentry stations, medic stations, seed silos, and fertiliser silos
- Mass Open Scarecrows
- Mass Wolf Traps (fill, collect, or both)

### Mass Fill (Structures)
- Refinery mass fill
- Cooking fire mass fill
- Kitchen mass fill
- Pub mass fill

### Mass Collect
- Collect all god tears at once
- Mass collect from beds, outhouses, offering shrines, passive shrines, compost, and harvest totems
- Mass clean poop and vomit

### Cost System
- Per-action or per-object cost mode
- Configurable gold and time costs
- Configurable faith reduction for mass Bless/Inspire
- Optional cost previews in interaction labels and follower wheel

## Installation

- Install [BepInExPack CultOfTheLamb](https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/BepInEx/BepInExPack_CultOfTheLamb/)
- Install [BepInEx Configuration Manager](https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/p1xel8ted/BepInEx_Configuration_Manager/)
- Place the plugin DLL in `.../Cult of the Lamb/BepInEx/plugins`

## Configuration

Press `F1` in game to open Configuration Manager.

## Notes

- Installing both Cult of QoL and Mass of the Lamb restores the pre-split combined behavior.
- If you only install Cult of QoL, mass action features are no longer included.
