[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Followers can be reborn, allowing you to change their names, looks etc.[/size]
[*][size=3]First one is free; subsequent ones on the same follower require Rebirth Tokens obtained from crusading (default 25, configurable).[/size]
[*][size=3]Source of the tokens is from killing enemies, destroying old bone piles, dungeon chests, offering shrines, and more.[/size]
[*][size=3]The drop chance defaults to 5% (configurable).[/size]
[*][size=3]The followers' XP carries over, but there is a 20% chance they lose some XP when reborn (configurable). There is a notification when this happens.[/size]
[*][size=3]Their old body stays; compost it, eat it. Up to you.[/size]
[*][size=3]Refine the tokens at your refineries from bones (default 15, configurable)![/size]
[*][size=3]Send your followers on a mission to get tokens![/size]
[*][size=3]Go on a quest yourself to get tokens![/size]
[*][size=3]All values are configurable via the in-game mod settings menu or the BepInEx config file.[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cultofthelamb/mods/31]Cult of the Lamb BepInEx 5 Pack[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/xhayper/COTL_API/]COTL API[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/p1xel8ted/ConfigurationManagerEnhanced/]Configuration Manager Enhanced[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Place the plugin DLLs into your "...\Cult of the Lamb\BepInEx\plugins" folder.[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Press F1 in game to open the configuration manager.[/size]
[*][size=3]The configuration file can be found in the "...\Cult of the Lamb\BepInEx\config" folder.[/size]
[/list]
