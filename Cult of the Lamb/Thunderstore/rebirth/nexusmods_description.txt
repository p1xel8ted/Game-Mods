[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[size=3][b]Rebirth Mechanic[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][size=3]Followers can be reborn, changing their name, appearance, and traits[/size]
[*][size=3]First rebirth is free; subsequent rebirths cost Rebirth Tokens (default: 25)[/size]
[*][size=3]XP carries over with configurable penalty (default: 20% chance to keep only 50% XP)[/size]
[*][size=3]Option to allow old/elderly followers to be reborn (disabled by default - old followers cannot be reborn)[/size]
[*][size=3]Unique followers (Webber, Sozo, Ratau, etc.) can retain their original skin and traits when reborn[/size]
[*][size=3]Their old body stays after rebirth; compost it, eat it, up to you[/size]
[/list]

[size=3][b]Token Sources[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][size=3][b]Enemy drops[/b]: 5% chance, 1-2 tokens per drop (configurable)[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Dungeon chests[/b]: 5% chance, 4-7 tokens per chest (configurable)[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Refinery[/b]: Craft from 15 bones, takes ~4.3 minutes (configurable)[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Missions[/b]: Send followers to collect 15-25 tokens, 50% mission appearance chance (configurable)[/size]
[*][size=3][b]Quests[/b]: Go on a quest yourself to collect 15-25 tokens[/size]
[/list]

[size=3]All values are configurable via the in-game mod settings menu (F1) or the BepInEx config file.[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cultofthelamb/mods/31]Cult of the Lamb BepInEx 5 Pack[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/xhayper/COTL_API/]COTL API[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/p1xel8ted/BepInEx_Configuration_Manager/]BepInEx Configuration Manager[/url] (for in-game configuration)[/size]
[*][size=3]Place the plugin DLLs into your "...\Cult of the Lamb\BepInEx\plugins" folder.[/size]
[/list]

[size=4][b]Configuration[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Press F1 in game to open the configuration manager.[/size]
[*][size=3]The configuration file can be found in the "...\Cult of the Lamb\BepInEx\config" folder.[/size]
[/list]
