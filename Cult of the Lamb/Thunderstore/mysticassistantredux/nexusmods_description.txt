[size=4][b]Mystic Assistant Redux[/b][/size]

[size=3]A continuation of [url=https://github.com/Ciarenni/COTL_MysticAssistant]Ciarenni's Mystic Assistant[/url] mod, updated for the latest game version.[/size]

[size=3]Buy specific rewards from the Mystic Shop instead of spinning the wheel. All agency, no RNG.[/size]

[size=4][b]Features[/b][/size]

[size=3]After beating the base game, visit the Mystic Shop and use the [b]secondary interaction[/b] for "Mystic Assistant" to open the shop.[/size]

[size=3][b]Available Items (configurable God Tear cost, default 1)[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][size=3]All 6 necklace types (Dark, Light, Gold Skull, Demonic, Loyalty, Missionary)[/size]
[*][size=3]Crystal Doctrine Stones (up to 24, or 28 with Woolhaven DLC)[/size]
[*][size=3]Talisman Pieces (up to 12)[/size]
[*][size=3]Follower Skins (random from unlockable pool)[/size]
[*][size=3]Decorations (random from unlockable pool)[/size]
[*][size=3]Tarot Cards (random from unlockable pool)[/size]
[*][size=3]Relics (random from unlockable pool)[/size]
[*][size=3]Black Gold (100 coins per purchase)[/size]
[/list]

[size=3][b]Exclusive Content[/b][/size]
[list]
[*][size=3]5 Apple Arcade follower skins, 2 clothing items, 1 fleece, 5 decorations[/size]
[/list]

[size=3][b]Optional Content (Config)[/b][/size]
[size=3]The following items are disabled by default since they're obtainable through normal gameplay. Enable them in the configuration manager under "Extra Content":[/size]
[list]
[*][size=3]5 Woolhaven DLC necklaces (Death's Door, Winter, Frozen, Targeted, Weird) — requires Woolhaven DLC[/size]
[*][size=3]31 boss follower skins — normally unlocked by defeating bosses[/size]
[/list]

[size=3][b]God Tear Cost[/b][/size]
[size=3]The cost per item is configurable (1-10, default 1). Increasing the cost requires confirmation and is locked per save — it can only go up, never down. The cost can only be changed while a save is loaded. The lock is automatically cleared when a save is deleted or overwritten.[/size]

[size=3][b]Overbuy Warnings[/b][/size]
[size=3]The shop will warn you before purchasing items beyond their normal limits (talisman pieces, dark/light necklaces). Click again to confirm.[/size]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/cultofthelamb/mods/31]Cult of the Lamb BepInEx 5 Pack[/url][/size]
[*][size=3]Install [url=https://thunderstore.io/c/cult-of-the-lamb/p/p1xel8ted/BepInEx_Configuration_Manager/]BepInEx Configuration Manager[/url] (for in-game configuration)[/size]
[*][size=3]Place the plugin DLL into your "...\Cult of the Lamb\BepInEx\plugins" folder.[/size]
[/list]
